about myself

My real name is Daniel, im 16 years old, live in los angeles, and love taking photos. I have been taking photos for over 2 years, capturing places, people, and nature. I first got introduced to photography by my dad. I was somewhat familiar with photography because I have always been into making videos. The way i discovered more about photography was when my sister was born. Around that time my dad took us to the swamp meet to go look around. He had borrowed a camera from one of my uncles, which he then gave to me and told me to take pictures. After the day i really enjoyed looking back and viewing the photos i took, ranking them, and sharing them. Since then i have found a passion in photography, a passion that i will have forever...
